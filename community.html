Nergy Student Council

The Nergy Student Council is a dynamic hub for students, encouraging connections, sharing achievements, and participating in exciting contests.

  • Opportunities to network with peers and industry professionals

The Nergy Academic Council is a group of educators and industry experts dedicated to enhancing academic experience in commerce.

  • Access to the latest research and trends in commerce education

  • Opportunities to collaborate with other educators

  • Access to resources that can help improve teaching methods

Nergy One Alliance
